Opportunities for Graduate Study with CHICAS

PhD Statistics and Epidemiology

A CHICAS PhD Doctorate is three years of full-time research working on a statistical and computational project, developing methodology and/or an application in spatial statistics, longitudinal data, or genetics. Many of our current projects highlight the work of our PhD students.

For full details about funding and applications see the University PhD Schemes page.

MSc Health Data Science

Our Masters in Health Data Science will teach you the combination of statistical thinking and computer science in a health context which is becoming increasingly fundamental in tackling public health problems across the world.

For full details including fees, scholarships, and applications see the University Postgraduate Courses page. You may also find links to additional funding opportunities on our funding page.

LSTM & LU MRC Doctoral Training Partnership PhD

CHICAS also supervises students admitted to the Liverpool School of Tropical Medicine and Lancaster University DTP.
