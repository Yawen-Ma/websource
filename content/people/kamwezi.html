Richard Kamwezi is part of the CHICAS group where he is studying for his PhD in Statistics and Epidemiology. His PhD focuses on developing novel methodologies for analysing malaria antibody data to inform malaria control and it is supervised by Dr Emanuele Giorgi.

Prior to joining CHICAS, Richard worked with the Malawi-Liverpool Wellcome Trust Clinical Research Programme in Malawi and studied for an MSc in Epidemiology at the London School of Hygiene and Tropical Medicine.
