Matt is currently a postdoctoral researcher in the CHICAS research centre, in the Lancaster Medical School. The focus of his research is the development and application of spatio-temporal disease models. His work is part of the larger BEPREP project.

He has an interdisciplinary background and completed his PhD in Computer Science / Engineering at the University of Central Lancashire.
