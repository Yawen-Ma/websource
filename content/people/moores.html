Sam joined Lancaster and CHICAS in 2022 as a biostatistics lecturer. Following an undergraduate degree in Edinburgh, he went on to gain a PhD at the University of Bath for work on the spread of infections on networks with Professor Tim Rogers.

Over the course of the COVID-19 pandemic Sam worked as a Research fellow alongside Professor Matt Keeling at the University of Warwick; here he produced modelling work that helped to shape the UK's pandemic response, informing vaccine prioritisation and the roadmap out of lockdown.
