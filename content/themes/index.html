CHICAS Research Themes

As a health research group, our mission is to understand, prevent, and treat different health conditions. We focus on a range of research themes, including epidemiology, health services research, infectious disease modeling, and population health data. By studying the distribution and determinants of disease in populations, improving the delivery and accessibility of healthcare services, simulating the spread and impact of infectious diseases, and analyzing information about the health and wellbeing of entire populations, we aim to make significant contributions to the field of public health. Through our research, we strive to identify patterns, disparities, and trends in health, and inform the development of targeted interventions to improve health outcomes for all.
