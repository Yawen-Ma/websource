CHICAS Research Themes

We are a research group with internationally leading expertise in epidemiology and biostatistics. We work on a range of research projects that focus on infectious disease modeling, geospatial statistics and population health data. Through our research, we strive to identify patterns, disparities, and trends in health, and inform the development of targeted interventions to improve health outcomes for all.
